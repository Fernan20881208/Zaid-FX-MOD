# Zaid-FX-MOD

Zaid-FX-MOD adds color grading and lightweight screen effects to Geometry Dash on Android.

## Included controls

- Brightness and exposure
- Contrast, saturation and gamma
- Vignette and sharpening
- Chromatic aberration
- Highlight glow
- Tone mapping
- Global effect intensity

The included presets are **Default**, **Cinematic**, **Vibrant**, **Dark**, **Retro** and **Glow**. Changing a slider switches the selected preset to **Custom**.

The effect is applied in one fullscreen shader pass immediately before Android presents the frame. It does not change gameplay physics, hitboxes or level data.

For bug reports, use the GitHub Issues link and include your device, GPU, Android, Geometry Dash and Geode versions.
